Anchor Calibration History

Early exploratory references within COSIGO development used an approximate relationship of ~21 mg silver per fresh egg based on limited initial sampling.

As broader historical averaging and modern market comparisons were examined, the long-term relationship was found to be closer to ~88 mg silver per egg.

For clarity, stability, and practical accounting across the system, the working anchor was standardized at 1 fresh egg = 100 COSIGO.

  • Initial exploratory estimate: ~21 mg silver per egg
  • Expanded historical analysis: ~88 mg silver per egg
  • System anchor selected: 100 COSIGO per egg

This calibration reflects refinement through expanded data while preserving continuity with real-world goods and long-term silver purchasing power relationships.
